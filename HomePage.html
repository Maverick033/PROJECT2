WELCOME

The fraternity and sorority community at Miami University - Ohio has one of the richest Greek histories in the nation, recognized by many as the "Mother of Fraternities." Miami Greeks are constantly being honored for their academic, philanthropic, athletic, leadership, community, and social achievements on the campus, local, and national levels. With the advantage of having 40 social Greek organizations, there are endless opportunities for everyone to become involved in Greek life. The Interfraternity Council (IFC), the governing body of all 22 on-campus fraternities, is comprised of one representative from each fraternity chapter and ten annually elected IFC Executive Board Officers. The Interfraternity Council is responsible for communication between on-campus fraternities and the school, regulation of recruitment, organizing Greek Service Week, establishing policies (judicial hearings, social policy, and more), Greek Week and much more.
